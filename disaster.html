Disaster Preparedness

Being prepared is key to staying safe during a disaster. Here are some steps you can take:

  • Create an emergency kit with essential supplies
  • Develop a family communication plan
  • Know your evacuation routes and nearest shelters
  • Stay informed about potential risks in your area
  • Participate in community disaster preparedness drills
